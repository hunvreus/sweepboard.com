Why Octokan?

We love GitHub, but its issue queue can get messy:

  • Issues get buried and people tend to focus more on the newest issues.
  • It's hard to prioritize when issues are displayed as a flat list.
  • Navigating issues is cumbersome. Switching threads and adding new issues quickly turn into page refresh galore.
